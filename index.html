  • How does the free trial work?

    • When you start your trial with Bonsai you'll receive full, unlimited access to all of Bonsai's Workflow or Workflow Plus Features! You will need to enter your credit card information to begin your trial, but don't worry - we won't charge you anything until the subscription ends! If you wish to cancel at any time during the trial period, you can do so through your Subscriptions Settings Page.

  • What is included in the subscription?

    • The subscription includes access to all premium features, regular updates, and dedicated customer support. You can explore a wide range of tools designed to enhance your productivity and streamline your workflow.

  • How can I manage my subscription?

    • You can manage your subscription through the Subscription Settings Page, where you can update your payment information, change your plan, or cancel your subscription at any time. Our support team is always available to assist you with any questions.

  • What payment methods are accepted?

    • We accept a variety of payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. You can choose the most convenient option for you during the checkout process. If you have any issues with payment, please contact our support team for assistance.
